##### Notes
>The verb here, 満たす, primarily means "to satisfy, to fulfill", etc. but also has the secondary meaning of "to fill (e.g. a hole)”. To be consistent with the translation of the novel I went with fulfill. ^1

>Minor wordplay in that the phrase in Japanese can mean "I don't have enough money", when in context it's clear that he means money is not enough to "know love" or to fulfill him. ^2
