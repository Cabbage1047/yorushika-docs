##### Notes
>The song is inspired by “Town Musicians of Bremen” by the Grimm Brothers. There are two variants of this fairytale, the western-prominent version is easily accessible today(Town Musicians of Bremen) while the eastern-prominent version(supposedly based on “Herr Korbes” by the same author) is not. The Eastern-prominent version is seemingly the inspiration for this.
>
>While one might find meaning in the song/title, n-buna stated that it does not have any meaning in this [tweet](https://twitter.com/nbuna_staff/status/1543891820249509888). But it might still be a reference to the song’s lyrics or the fairy tale’s meaning.

>  “Bước chân của những con người sống trong thời hiện đại. Thực tế, trên đời này không có gì kinh khủng đến mức phải chết cả.
> 
> Truyện cổ Grimm “Những nhạc công thành Bremen”
